Profile

I'm a PhD student at Ghent University since 2013 advised by Chris Develder and Thomas Demeester, I'm staying at the INTEC-IBCN group.

My interests are in natural language processing and information extraction. I have been working on topic models, keyphrase extraction, knowledge base population and sequence-to-sequence models.
